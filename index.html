How the Couples Therapy Assistant Works

A structured system designed to help couples improve communication, resolve conflict, and strengthen their relationship in real time.

Step 1: Therapist-Guided Access

The platform is designed for use through licensed therapists. Couples receive access through their therapist, ensuring structured and professional guidance.

Step 2: Real-Time Communication Support

During everyday interactions, couples can use the app to get AI-guided responses that help them navigate difficult conversations, reduce conflict, and improve understanding.

Step 3: Daily Emotional Check-Ins

Users track their emotional state and relationship health through structured check-ins. This creates awareness and helps identify patterns over time.

Step 4: Therapist Integration

Therapists can monitor shared insights, assign exercises, and track progress between sessions, improving overall outcomes and engagement.

Step 5: Continuous Improvement

By combining real-time support with therapist guidance, couples build stronger communication habits and long-term relationship stability.
